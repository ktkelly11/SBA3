Secondhand Instruments

Welcome to the secondhand instrument donation site, where we recognize the importance of music education, especially in a public school setting, and try to get as many no longer used instruments into the hands of students who need them. This website is inspired by my wonderful mother, Margaret. She is so giving and always thinking of others. She is constantly looking for or thrifting instruments that maybe just need a little fixing up. She does this because my brother is a public school music teacher in Vermont, and his wife, my sister-in-law is a music therapist. With two musicians in our family who know of lots of students in need of instruments, it is wonderful to help support those interested in studying music.
